How To Play

Goal of the game: explode all the balloons that appear on the screen before time runs out.

  • To pop a balloon, point the peephole at the balloon and click. The balloons can have different colors.
  • Some balloons may move faster than others, so keep an eye out!
  • As you advance in level, the speed and number of balloons will increase.
  • The score is based on the number of balloons popped. Special colored balloons can provide additional benefits.

Good luck and have fun!
